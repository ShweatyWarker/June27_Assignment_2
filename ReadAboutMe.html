My Journey with Logic Pro

My experience with Logic Pro has been a rollercoaster. I absolutely hated Logic Pro 8. The interface felt clunky and unintuitive. It just didnâ€™t work with how I liked to create music.

Everything changed when Logic Pro 9 was released. It was smooth, responsive, and inspiring. I fell in love with the process of making music again.

I used Ultrabeat for drums and samples. It was my go-to tool for creating rhythms and grooves. I also had a great set of plugins that really brought my beats to life.

When Logic Pro X came out, things went downhill. Most of my plugins stopped working. I couldn't use the tools I had grown to depend on. The update felt like a step backward.

Eventually, I stopped making beats. The passion faded when the workflow I loved was taken away. Still, I look back on those creative years with pride.
